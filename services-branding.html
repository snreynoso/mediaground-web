Branding and visual design

Creating a strong identity is the first step to present your product to the world. Here’s what we can do.

Naming and voice tone

To begin building a brand identity, you’ll first need to clearly identify your company’s mission, values and objectives. What sets you apart from competitors, and why do customers choose you? The answer to these questions is the foundation of your brand identity.

Logo and illustrations

Then we can let creativity run free: color palette, typographies, logos and illustrations. Generating a coherent visual language will enhance the brand, making it strong and solid.

Product packaging and presentation

Finally, we will take care of the presentation of the brand, applying its elements both on different physical supports (such as packaging, billboards and business cards) and digital ones.
